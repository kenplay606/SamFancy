## 📄 Page Sections (In Order)

### 1. **Hero Section**
- Full-screen with background image
- Large "SAM FANCY" title
- Tagline: "A Chinatown Story in the Heart of LKF"
- **Two clear CTAs**:
  - `SEE MENU` (red pill button)
  - `FIND US` (white outline button)

### 2. **Story Section** (id: `story`)
- Clean, centered layout
- 2-3 sentences about the San Francisco/Chinatown origin
- Simple fade-in animation

### 3. **Spaces Section** (id: `spaces`)
- **Two simple cards** side-by-side (stacks on mobile)
- **3rd Floor - The Hangout**: Wine icon, red accent
- **4th Floor - The Speakeasy**: Film icon, gold accent
- Rounded corners (16px), subtle borders
- Hover effect: border changes to red

### 4. **Menu Section** (id: `menu-section`)
- Uses the existing dark-themed interactive menu
- Tabbed interface: Drinks | Cocktails | Food | Wine
- Clean, organized layout

### 5. **Visit Section** (id: `visit`)
- **2x2 Grid Layout** (becomes single column on mobile)
  1. **Happy Hour**: $30 | $40 | $50 (6-9 PM)
  2. **Location**: Address with link to Google Maps
  3. **Hours**: Mon-Sun, 6 PM - Late
  4. **Private Events**: Email contact
- Google Maps embed below grid
- All cards have rounded corners and hover effects

### 6. **Reviews Section**
- **Simple horizontal grid** (4 cards on desktop, stacks on mobile)
- Each card shows:
  - 5 gold stars
  - Short quote
  - Author name
- No carousel - just clean, scannable layout

### 7. **Footer**
- Navigation links (Story | Spaces | Menu | Visit)
- Instagram icon
- **Back to Top** button (red circle with arrow)
- Copyright and address

## 🎨 Design System

### **Colors**
- Background: `#000000` (black) and `#18181B` (zinc-950)
- Cards: `#09090B` (zinc-950) with `#27272A` (zinc-800) borders
- Primary accent: `#DC143C` (chinatown-red)
- Secondary accent: `#D4AF37` (gold-accent)
- Text: White, gray-300, gray-400

### **Typography**
- Headlines: Anton font (font-headline)
- Body: Inter font
- All uppercase for buttons and nav links

### **Buttons**
- Pill-shaped (`rounded-full`)
- Red fill with white text
- Hover: Darker red
- Smooth transitions (300ms)

### **Cards**
- Rounded corners (`rounded-2xl` = 16px)
- Border: 1px solid zinc-800
- Hover: Border changes to chinatown-red
- Padding: 8 (2rem)

### **Animations**
- Simple fade-in on scroll
- No complex effects
- Smooth scroll behavior
- Subtle opacity changes on hover

---

## 📱 Mobile Responsiveness

### **Navigation**
- Hamburger menu icon on mobile
- Expands to show all links vertically
- Closes when link is clicked

### **Sections**
- Hero: Full-screen, buttons stack vertically
- Story: Single column, centered
- Spaces: Cards stack vertically
- Menu: Tabs become 2x2 grid on small screens
- Visit: 2x2 grid becomes single column
- Reviews: 4 cards become vertical list
- Footer: Stacks vertically

### **Touch Targets**
- All buttons minimum 44x44px
- Easy to tap on mobile devices
